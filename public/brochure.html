About Us
Cultivating Excellence for the Global Market

Agrovia Enterprise is a premier merchant exporter based in Gujarat, India, specializing in top-tier dehydrated onion, dehydrated garlic, authentic ground spices, and pure natural honey.

Founded with a vision to connect local farms with international markets, we ensure that every flake, granule, powder, and jar of honey retains its natural essence. Our state-of-the-art processing facilities and stringent quality control measures guarantee that our products meet the highest global standards.

  • Ethically sourced from sustainable farms in Mahuva, Gujarat
  • Advanced cold-chain dehydration technology
  • Uncompromising hygiene & food safety standards
  • Timely global delivery via Mundra Port
  • Custom packaging & private labelling available
  • Bulk supply: 20-ft container = 18–20 MT capacity
